## What is AniNovel?

AniNovel is an Android app that lets you watch anime, read manga, and read novels — all from a single app. No switching between multiple apps anymore.

## Features

**Anime**
- Stream anime from a wide range of sources
- Powerful built-in video player with subtitle support, playback speed, and hardware decoding
- Track your watch progress on MyAnimeList, AniList, Kitsu, Shikimori, Simkl, and Bangumi

**Manga**
- Read manga from hundreds of sources
- Multiple reading modes and directions
- Download chapters for offline reading
- Track your reading progress

**Novels**
- Read novels from sources like RoyalRoad, NovelFull, MtlNovels, FreeWebNovel, and more
- Built-in Text-to-Speech with word-by-word highlighting
- Skip lines while listening using volume keys
- Novel library with favorites and read/unread tracking
- Swipe chapters to mark as read/unread

**General**
- Unified Browse section for Anime, Manga, and Novel sources
- Extension system for adding more sources
- Library organization with categories
- Light and dark themes
- Backup and restore your library
- Requires Android 8.0 or higher
